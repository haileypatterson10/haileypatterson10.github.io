Who is Hailey?

I work on the design desk of the Daily Tar Heelhere in Chapel Hill. This Paper is not technically affiliated with the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill but it is the primary way that the campus gets the news. At The Daily Tar Heel I produce graphics that can be used in the paper as well as design page layouts. I have been working here since November of 2022.

I also work as a Digital Content and Media Specialist for the UNC Romance Language Publications office. This is a work study position that I have held since September 2022. In this position I manage the publication's social medias, make graphics for events, and update their website.

Finally, I also work at Stone and Leaf cafe as a barista and cafe supervisor. In this position I present excellent customer service skills, remember recipies and make drinks, manage co-workers, and other neccisities when needed. I have worked in this position since May of 2022.

I graduated with my Associates of Science degree from Asheville-Buncombe Community College at the same time that I graduated with my highschool diploma (2021). I am now a junior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I am majoring in Information Science concentrating in web design and graphic design, with a studio art minor. My graduation year is 2025

At UNC I play Women's Club Team Handball. I have been playing since freshman year. We came in third place in college nationals 2022 and second in 2023. In 2022 I was selected to be one of three alternates for the USA Women's Junior National Handball Team. In highschool I played varsity soccer and varsity volleyball, and I captained in both.
